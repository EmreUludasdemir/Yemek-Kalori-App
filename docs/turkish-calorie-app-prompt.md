# Building a Turkish Calorie Tracking App with AI and Social Features

A Turkey-focused calorie tracking app with AI food recognition represents a significant market opportunity. No dominant local player exists, while international apps like MyFitnessPal and YAZIO offer poor Turkish food support.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: Flutter 3+ (iOS, Android, Web, Desktop)
- **Backend**: Supabase (PostgreSQL, Auth, Storage, Realtime)
- **AI Recognition**: Calorie Mama API + on-device TFLite
- **Nutrition Data**: FatSecret + TürKomp + Open Food Facts
- **Push Notifications**: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)
- **Image CDN**: ImageKit
- **State Management**: Riverpod
- **Charts**: fl_chart + percent_indicator

## MVP Cost: ~$34-54/month

- Supabase Pro: $25/month
- ImageKit: $9/month
- Firebase FCM: Free
- FatSecret: $0-19/month (Free tier available)

## Key Features

### Core Features
- AI-powered food recognition from photos
- Manual food search and logging
- Barcode scanning for packaged foods
- Daily calorie and macro tracking
- Water intake tracking
- Comprehensive Turkish food database

### Social Features
- Social feed with posts, likes, comments
- Follow/unfollow system
- Share meals and progress
- Achievements and gamification
- Streak tracking

### Turkish Market Focus
- Traditional Turkish dishes (döner, lahmacun, mantı, etc.)
- TürKomp integration (645 lab-analyzed Turkish foods)
- Cultural meal patterns (kahvaltı, iftar/sahur for Ramadan)
- Turkish barcode support (GS1 prefixes 868, 869)
